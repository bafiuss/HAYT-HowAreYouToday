Personal Diary

The personal diary allows users to express their thoughts and emotions in a private and secure space. Patients can add new thoughts to reflect on their feelings, track emotional patterns, and document their mental health journey. Users can also revisit past thoughts to gain insights into their progress and emotional well-being over time.

Daily Thoughts
🧑‍⚕️

Therapist & Appointments

Our platform allows users to connect with professional therapists for personalized support. Patients can easily browse available psychotherapists, view their profiles, and schedule appointments based on their preferences.

Guided Support
❓

Questionnaire

Our platform includes a DSM-5-based questionnaire designed to help users gain insights into their mental health. By answering a series of clinically validated questions, patients can assess their emotional and psychological well-being. The questionnaire provides an initial screening for potential mental health conditions, guiding users toward professional support if needed.

Screening Tool
💬

Therapist Chat

Our platform offers a secure and confidential chat feature that allows users to communicate directly with professional therapists. Whether you need immediate guidance or ongoing support, the chat provides a convenient way to discuss concerns, ask questions, and receive expert advice in real time. This feature ensures that users have easy access to professional help whenever they need it, fostering a continuous and supportive mental health journey.

Instant Help
