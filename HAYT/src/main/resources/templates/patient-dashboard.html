DEPRESSION

In the past week, how often have you felt sad, empty, or hopeless?

In the past week, how often have you lost interest or pleasure in activities that you previously found enjoyable?

In the past week, how often have you noticed a significant increase or decrease in your appetite?

In the past week, how often have you had difficulty falling asleep, staying asleep, or waking up too early?

In the past week, how often have you felt tired or lacked energy?

In the past week, how often have you felt guilt, worthlessness, or been overly critical of yourself?

In the past week, how often have you found it difficult to concentrate, think, or make decisions?

ANXIETY

In the past week, how often have you had excessive and persistent thoughts of worry or anxiety about daily life (e.g., work, relationships, health)?

In the past week, how often have you felt restless or unable to relax?

In the past week, how often have you felt tired or exhausted even after small efforts?

In the past week, how often have you found it difficult to concentrate or had your mind go blank?

In the past week, how often have you felt more irritable than usual?

In the past week, how often have you experienced muscle tension, cramps, or pain without a specific medical cause?

In the past week, how often have you had difficulty sleeping due to persistent worries or anxieties?

FUNCTIONAL IMPAIRMENT

In the past week, how much have symptoms of anxiety or depression affected your ability to work or study?

In the past week, how much have you struggled to maintain or engage in social interactions (e.g., with friends, family, or others)?

In the past week, how much have you found it difficult to enjoy leisure time or activities you usually like?
